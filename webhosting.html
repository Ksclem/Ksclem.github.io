Web Hosting

Bluehost

Basic is $2.95 ($11.99 after one year) a month with ten websites. Plus is $4.95 ($16.99 after one year) a month with twenty websites. They offer 24/7 hosting support. Trustpilot.com gave them a four star rating. Based on reviews, this is a very reliable host, but slightly more expensive.Their bandwidth is unmetered and a free domain is included with all plans. You can have 5 emails with it.

GoDaddy

Economy is $9.99 a month and Deluxe is $13.99 a month. Economy gives one website and Deluxe gives twenty. They advertise 40% faster loading pages and have 24/7 security monitoring. Trustpilot.com gave them a four and a half star rating. They have daily backups as well. They also have a website builder that is easy to use and a free domain with all plans.

Hostinger

Business is $13.99 a month and Premium is $11.99 a month both for 100 websites. It also has 24/7 suppport and AI assistance plugin. Trustpilot.com gave them a four and a half star rating.Reviews show that this is a beginner friendly host as well. There is a website builder tool included. You can have 100 emails with it and a free domain is only offered with the Premium plan or higher.
